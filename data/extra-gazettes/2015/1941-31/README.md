# [1941/31] Department of Fiscal Policy - Order to direct that a duty at the rate of a Rs. 1850.00 per proof litre on Molasses, Palmyrah, Coconut and Processed Arrack and a duty at the rate of Rs. 2030.00 on every proof litre on country made Foreign spirits manufactured in Sri Lanka shall be levied and a duty at the rate of Rs. 160.00 shall be imposed on malt liquor of less than 5% and above of absolute strength and a duty shall not be levied on such brewery issued for the use of any Diplomatic Mission with effect from 21.11.2015

**Date:** 2015-11-20
